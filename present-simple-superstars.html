🌱 What is the Present Simple?

We use the Present Simple for things that are true now, happen again and again (habits & routines), or are always true (facts).

🕒 When do we use it?

🌅 Daily routines — I wake up at 7.
🌍 Facts — The sun rises in the east.
🔁 Habits — She reads every night.
❤️ Likes/dislikes — We love ice cream.

✅ Positive

⚽ I play football. ✔
🎹 She plays the piano. +s

❌ Negative

🥕 I don't like carrots. ✘
📺 He doesn't watch TV. ✘

❓ Questions

🍕 Do you like pizza? ?
🏫 Does she go to school? ?

⭐ The Golden Rule

Add s / es to the verb with he, she, it. Add es after -o, -ch, -sh, -ss, -x (go→goes, watch→watches). Consonant + y → ies (cry→cries).
